## Seeding Notes

### VRS Seeding Priority

- Teams with Valve Regional Standings (VRS) ranking are seeded **first**
- Teams are ordered by their **VRS points** from the February 2nd, 2026 leaderboard
- Only teams appearing in both the Season 4 team list AND the VRS leaderboard are VRS-seeded

### Non-VRS Teams

- Non-VRS team seeding was completed on **February 15th, 2026**
- Remaining teams were seeded based on ESEA League Division and Win/Loss record

### Tournament Lock Notice

**As of February 16th, 2026**, the tournament is **two weeks out** from the start date. To ensure **fair play and competitive integrity** for all participants, seeding finalization has been completed and no further seeding or team changes will be made.

## Team Requirements

### Roster Rules

- **Maximum roster:** 8 players (5 starters, 2 substitutes, 1 coach)
- **Minimum core players:** 3 original core players must play per match
- **Roster changes:** Allowed during Group Stage, locked for Playoffs
- **All players must reside in North America**

### Eligibility

- ✅ FACEIT Anti-Cheat required
- ✅ No active VAC or platform bans
- ✅ Team captain designated from core players
- ✅ Entry fee: $100 per team
- ✅ Payment deadline: February 23rd, 2026 @ 8:00 PM EST
